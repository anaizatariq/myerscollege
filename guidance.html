Empowering every student's path — from school to the world's top universities

University Placements

Shaping Futures,
One Student at a Time

Career and future planning are significant, as acknowledged by Myer's College. We comprehend how intimidating the next stage of formal education can be, and we are conscious of the ambiguities and worries that students frequently encounter.

We provide guidance and counselling services to any student who requests support. Our counselling section helps students understand their course options, assists with personal statement writing, and provides direction for university applications. Numerous career events are planned so students can hear from experts across a variety of disciplines.

What We Provide

Counseling Services

Academic Counseling

Guidance on subject selection, study techniques, exam preparation and academic planning. Our counselors help students identify their strengths and sharpen areas that need improvement.

Personal Counseling

Confidential support for personal issues, stress management and emotional well-being. A safe space to discuss concerns and receive professional guidance through life's challenges.

Career Guidance

Career counseling sessions to explore options, university admissions and professional development. We guide students in selecting the right path with expert input from industry professionals.

Behavioural Support

Programs for character building, conflict resolution and positive behavioural habits. We help build resilience, discipline and a strong moral compass in every student.

Parent Counseling

Regular meetings and workshops for parents to understand their child's progress and strengthen the home-school partnership for optimal student development.

University Placement

Dedicated support for university applications nationally and internationally. Our counselors assist with the full process — personal statements, applications and interview preparation.
