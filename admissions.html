Admissions Open — Starting March

The admission process for classes Kindergarten to 10th begins in March each year. Students admitted in March attend special two-month summer coaching to align with our August start.

Overview

Admission Process

Admissions at Myer's are limited and highly competitive. Only 24 students are accommodated in each section, with each class having only two sections. It is easier to get a child admitted in the Pre-school (ages 2–4) as seats are very limited in higher classes — applicants are frequently placed on a waiting list.

Students admitted in March attend specially designed coaching classes to strengthen their English language and Mathematical skills before the August term begins — ensuring every new student is fully prepared from day one.

How To Apply

Steps to Get Admission

1

Step One

Obtain the Prospectus

Collect the Myer's College prospectus from the Information Desk and read it thoroughly to understand the academic structure, values and requirements.

2

Step Two

Complete the Application Forms

Fill in the enclosed forms carefully and attach all required documents — National ID Card copy, photographs, Birth Certificate, and any previous academic records.

3

Step Three

Register at the Admissions Office

Submit your completed application forms with all required documents and the registration fee at the Admissions Office to receive your registration confirmation.

4

Step Four

Schedule an Interview & Test

Arrange an interview and entrance test appointment directly with the Admissions Office at a mutually convenient date and time.

5

Step Five

Attend the Interview & Assessment

Your child will be interviewed and assessed by the relevant faculty. The test is designed to gauge current ability and readiness for Myer's academic standards.

6

Step Six — Final

Secure Admission & Complete the File

Upon passing the assessment, pay the admission fee and obtain your unique admission number. Complete your child's file as instructed by the office — welcome to Myer's!
