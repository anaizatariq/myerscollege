Facilities

World-class facilities for an enriching educational experience

Infrastructure

Academic Facilities

Myer's College is equipped with modern amenities to provide the best learning environment for all students.

Academic

Class Rooms

Spacious and well-lit classrooms with whiteboards and overhead projectors for multi-colour teaching presentations. Most windows offer a scenic view — never letting boredom set in.

Technology

Computer Lab

Fully equipped with a Local Area Network, scanning and printing stations. Myer's is committed to keeping pace with the rapidly changing technology landscape and integrating computer usage into school life.

Science

Physics, Chemistry & Biology Labs

Fully equipped and spacious science labs for all three disciplines. Learning through practicals is strongly encouraged so students gain a deep, hands-on understanding of natural science.

Knowledge

Library

A growing collection of books across all subjects with full Internet access. Reading books and discussing them with peers is an integral part of Myer's Culture, promoting innovation and broad vision.

Physical Education

Sports & Activities

We believe physical education is as vital as academics. Myer's offers a comprehensive range of sports and recreational activities for all students.

Swimming

Health experts agree that swimming exercises maximum body muscles. At Myer's we have built a competition-size swimming pool. The season runs from April through October, culminating in the annual Swimming Gala.

April – October Season

Badminton

Badminton courts with all essentials are provided to facilitate female students interested in the game. The Qamar Jehan Begum Memorial Badminton Tournament is held every April.

Annual Memorial Tournament

Horse Riding

Horse riding is not only an excellent exercise but also builds self-esteem and character. Our Riding Club offers a full training course for students as well as outsiders, with plans for a tent-pegging team.

Open to Students & Outsiders

Cricket

We facilitate all students interested in cricket with a dedicated field and full net-practice equipment. A specialist coach provides structured training sessions throughout the season.

Specialist Coach Provided

Football

Football season runs from October through April, with competitions between the five school houses. The annual General Iftikhar Khan Memorial Tournament is held in the first half of April. A full-size ground and specialist coach are provided.

October – April Season • 5 Houses
